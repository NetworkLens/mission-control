## Mission Control

A simple and fun chore-tracking Android app designed for everyone, presented with a cool space theme.

## Features

*   **Commander Login:** Start by entering your name to begin your mission.
*   **Chore List:** View your list of current missions (chores).
*   **Add Chores:** Add new chores to your list from a predefined set of tasks.
*   **Complete Chores:** Mark chores as complete and see a star light up.
*   **Delete Chores:** Remove chores from your list.
*   **Progress Tracker:** Watch your rocket fly across the screen as you complete your chores!
